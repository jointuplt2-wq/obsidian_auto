Quiz - Lesson 1: Who Are You?

#### Start the Quiz to find out

*What do you think of this lesson? We want to hear from you.*

*We have a new comment system. Here is how it works:*

1. *Write your comment in the box.*
2. *Under the box, you can see four images for social media accounts. They are for Disqus, Facebook, Twitter and Google.*
3. *Click on one image and a box appears. Enter the login for your social media account. Or you may create one on the Disqus system. It is a blue circle with “D” on it. It is free.*

*Each time you return to comment on the Learning English site, you can use your account and see your comments and replies to them.*
