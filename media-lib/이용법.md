## 🎬 Media Extended — Step by Step

### Step 1️⃣ — Open a video

- Make a new note (or open today's note)
- **Paste a YouTube link** (or VOA video link) on a line, e.g.:
    
    ```
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...
    ```
    
- The video player should appear in Obsidian 🎥

> 💡 Tip: Start with a short VOA Learning English video (easy + clear!) 🎧

---

### Step 2️⃣ — Play and listen

- Click ▶️ **Play**
- Listen until you hear a **sentence you like** (or a new word)

---

### Step 3️⃣ — Save a timestamp ⭐

- When you hear a good part, click the **⭐ star button** in the player toolbar
- It inserts a **timestamped link** into your note automatically, like:
    
    ```
    - [00:45] 
    ```
    
- Now **type the sentence** you heard next to it:
    
    ```
    - [00:45] "The story is all over the place."
    ```
    

---

### Step 4️⃣ — Jump back 🖱️

- Later, **click the [00:45] link**
- The video **jumps to that exact moment** → listen again! 🔁

---

### Step 5️⃣ — Repeat & collect 📚

- Do this for 3–5 sentences per video
- You build a personal list of English expressions you can **replay anytime!**
