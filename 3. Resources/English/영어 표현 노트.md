# 영어 표현 노트 (English Notes)

> 매일 배운 영어 표현과 교정을 여기에 모읍니다. 📚
> 새 표현이 생기면 아래에 계속 추가하세요!

## 📅 2026-07-01

### 오늘 배운 표현
| 한국어 | 영어 |
|--------|------|
| 대략 이해했어요 | I roughly understand. |
| 이제 이해했어요! | Now I understand! |
| ~부터 시작할게요 | I'll start from~ |
| 대략 / 약 (시간) | around (12:30) |

### 오늘 고친 문장 (Corrections)
- ❌ Now I approximately understand → ✅ Now I roughly understand.
- ❌ I do not know purpose of folders → ✅ I don't know **the** purpose of **the** folders.
- ❌ I had some idea → ✅ I **have** some **ideas**.

## 📅 2026-07-02

### 💬 오늘의 대화 (Q&A)
| 🤖 너의 질문 | 🙋 나의 대답 |
|--------------|--------------|
| What did you do this morning? | I ate kimchi, cucumber salad, and soft bean curd soup for lunch. |
| Did you cook the lunch yourself? | The restaurant cooks made it. |
| Do you eat at this restaurant often? | I eat here often because it's in my care home. |
| Do you have a favorite meal at your care home? | Yes, I love the chicken dish they serve every week. |
| What kind of chicken dish is it? | It's grilled chicken with sauce. |
| What kind of sauce is it? | I'm not sure what kind of sauce it is. |
| What other foods do you enjoy? | I like bulgogi and japchae. |
| Can you make bulgogi or japchae yourself? | I can't make either of them, so I prefer to eat them in my room. |
| (keyboard broke) | My keyboard isn't working, so I use the on-screen keyboard. |

### ✅ 바른 표현 (잘 쓴 문장)
- **It's grilled chicken with sauce.** — 완벽! 🎉
- **I like bulgogi and japchae.** — 완벽! 🎉
- **I eat here often because it's in my care home.** — 아주 좋음 👏

### ❌ 틀린 단어와 수정본 (Corrections)
- ❌ utill → ✅ **until** (L 하나)
- ❌ I would like to English conversation → ✅ I'd like to **have** an English conversation (would like to + **동사**)
- ❌ I ate lunch kimchi, cucumber salad... → ✅ I ate kimchi, cucumber salad, and soft bean curd soup **for lunch**.
- ❌ Restuarant are cookers → ✅ The **restaurant cooks made** it.
- ❌ I love the chiken dish serve → ✅ I love the **chicken** dish **they serve**.
- ❌ The screen has displayed answer → ✅ The answer **is displayed** on the screen.
- ❌ I didn't (불완전) → ✅ **I'm not sure** what kind of sauce it is.
- ❌ I can't make both → ✅ I can't make **either** of them (부정문 → either)
- ❌ eat them at my room → ✅ eat them **in** my room
- ❌ My keyboard is out of order → ✅ My keyboard **isn't working** (개인 물건엔 out of order 보다 isn't working)
- ❌ I use keyboard on the screen → ✅ I'm using **the on-screen keyboard**
- ❌ Sometimes I'm use → ✅ Sometimes I **use** (sometimes=습관 → 현재형, I'm 안 씀)
- ❌ Oh, time over → ✅ Oh, **time's up!**

## 📅 2026-07-03

### 💬 오늘의 대화 (Q&A)
| 🤖 너의 질문 | 🙋 나의 대답 |
|--------------|--------------|
| How did you improve the appearance of Obsidian? | I changed the color and the UI, and I added Claude. |
| Why did you add Claude? | I just copied what I saw on YouTube. |
| Do you often learn from YouTube? | Yes, I watch videos about new tools and Obsidian. |
| What new tool do you like? | I really like Claude Code. |
| What do you use Claude Code for? | I'd like to use it to organize my messages better. |
| How do you organize your notes now? | I use folders. |
| When will you practice listening? | I will practice listening in the morning. |
| Are you more focused in the morning? | Yes, I'm more focused in the morning. |
| What time do you wake up? | I usually wake up at 5:30 in the morning. |

### ✅ 바른 표현 (잘 쓴 문장)
- **I really like Claude Code.** — 완벽! 🎉
- **Yes, please recommend some listening-practice websites.** — 완벽! 🎉

### ❌ 틀린 단어와 수정본 (Corrections)
- ❌ I improved appeance of Obsidian in morning → ✅ I improved **the appearance** of Obsidian **this morning**.
- ❌ Today is same to yesterday → ✅ Today is **the same as** yesterday. (same **as**, not "to")
- ❌ I changed color, UI, and added Claude → ✅ I changed **the color and the UI, and I added** Claude.
- ❌ I just copied the Youtube → ✅ I just copied **what I saw on YouTube** (on YouTube, not "the YouTube")
- ❌ I watch new tools and Obsidian videos → ✅ I watch **videos about** new tools and Obsidian.
- ❌ messge / arrengement → ✅ **message** / **arrangement** (철자)
- ❌ Iuse folders → ✅ **I use** folders (띄어쓰기)
- ❌ hearing ability → ✅ **listening ability** (언어 학습엔 listening)
- ❌ I will hearing prectice → ✅ I will **practice listening** (동사) / **listening practice**(명사), 철자 practice

## 📝 문법 팁 모음

- **at** + 시각 (at 12:30) / **in** + 기간 (in the morning)
- 목록 마지막엔 **and**: "A, B, **and** C"
- 습관은 현재형: "I **study** English every day" (will 안 씀)
