**bud** _–n._ the beginnings of a flower or a leaf on the branch of a plant

**shoot** _–n._ new plant growth, especially from seeds or close to the ground as in the case with grass

**bloom** _–v._ the opening of a flower

**boring** _–adj._ not interesting

**tedious** _–adj._ tiresome because it takes a long time and is repetitive

**on-the-go** _–phrase_ having much high-spirited energy and movement
